Pencil leads: add ground graphite、clay、water, mix them, and they will become graphite dough. After that, put it into forming press machine, and it will turn the dough into rods which are knowing as leads. After drying and burning, the leads will become hard.
Pencil cases: first of all, grooves cut in wooden slat. After that, put leads into the slat and apply glue and the wood. And then, sandwich the slats. Finally, create smooth surface and put them into shaping machine in order to make the front sharp.
